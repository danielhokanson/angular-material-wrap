Validation Rules

  • Required: All range sliders are required
  • Range Logic: Start value must be less than end value
  • Bounds: Values must be within specified min/max ranges
  • Price Range: $0 - $1000 with $10 increments
  • Age Range: 18 - 100 years with 1 year increments
  • Temperature: -50°C - 100°C with 1°C increments
